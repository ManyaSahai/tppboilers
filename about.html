About TPP Boilers

Designer, Fabricator & Erector of Boiler Components & Equipments

We take this opportunity to introduce TPP Boilers Pvt. Ltd. as a trusted Designer, Fabricator, and Erector of high-quality boiler components and equipment made from steel plates, tubes, and pipes. We are a Small scale industry backed by our highly experienced CEO and CTO, along with a dedicated team of skilled workers — all of whom have roots in a reputed boiler manufacturing and erection company with over 50 years of legacy. This unique blend allows us to deliver the expertise of a large enterprise with the personalized service and cost-efficiency of a smaller organization. Our promoters and workforce collectively bring over 500 man-years of experience in fabrication, erection, bending, and welding of all types of plates, sections, tubes, and pipes — including seamless, ERW, and rifle-bore varieties. We specialize in working with a wide range of materials such as carbon steels, alloy steels, and stainless steels, across any size, length, or specification.

Our Facilities

State-of-Art Manufacturing Facility

Factory Space

We have a spacious 4000 Sq. Mtr. Factory equipped with state-of-art manufacturing equipment to ensure efficient production.

Manufacturing Capabilities

Our manufacturing facility is fully equipped to produce a wide range of industrial equipment using any grade or type of steel, with material thicknesses of up to 50 mm. We are capable of performing pipe bending operations for sizes up to 100 mm NB (114.3 mm OD) with wall thicknesses of Sch 160 (18 mm). Located on a 6,000 square meter area, our state-of-the-art factory houses modern fabrication equipment, enabling us to complete the entire production process in-house. This includes: · Plate and Tube Bending · Manual and Automated Welding · Pipe Coiling and Rolling · Component Assembly and Heat Treatment · Panel welding · Stud welding Our skilled and experienced workforce combines technical know-how with innovative practices, allowing us to achieve faster production timelines without compromising on the quality and reliability of the final product. We are supported by a strong team of managerial and technical experts, including engineers, supervisors, skilled, and semi-skilled workers. This team ensures smooth operations both at our factory and on project sites, with a consistent focus on design integrity, production standards, and stringent quality control. Specialized Fabrication Services: We also specialize in the production of: · Short Radius Tube Bends with a bending radius of R ≥ 0.6D · Y- Pieces · Water wall panels · Studded Pipes and Coils · Steam and Condensate Piping Systems · Sample coolers Every component is manufactured using precision machinery and proven processes such as section/pipe bending, pressing, rolling, drilling, welding, assembling, and heat treatment. Our continuous investment in technology and human resources ensures timely delivery while meeting the most demanding quality and performance standards.

Quality Control

Our in-house NDT lab with qualified personnel ensures all our products meet the highest quality standards. We perform 100% testing of all welds as per applicable codes.

Our Quality Commitment

Quality Assurance

Developed over more than two decades of our fabrication experience, our stringent Quality Control Process serializes and subjects all operations to 100% checks and thorough recording for complete traceability. We primarily focus on raw materials, welding joint integrity (verified by radiography/X-rays), dimensional accuracy, and comprehensive assembly pressure testing, concluding with meticulous painting and packing. We consistently exceed the requirements of leading material and fabrication codes including ASME, BSS, DIN, VGB, JIS, NF, GOST, ISO, and EN.

Certifications

Our manufacturing processes and products are certified to meet various international standards including ISO, ASME, and IBR, ensuring reliability and safety.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuous improvement through regular training, technology upgrades, and process optimization to deliver better products and services.
